]]> Responsibilities:

1 – Strategic Planning Support

  • Advise the Chief Executive Officer on the development and execution of strategic plans to achieve the organization’s overall objectives.
  • Conduct research and prepare analytical reports to support strategic decision-making and business development initiatives.

2 – Project Management and Oversight

  • Directly manage and oversee assigned projects to ensure progress, quality, and optimal use of resources in line with approved plans.
  • Anticipate potential risks during project implementation and propose appropriate mitigation measures.

3 – Internal and External Relations

  • Act as a key liaison between the Chief Executive Officer and subsidiaries, departments, ensuring timely and effective communication of decisions, information, and initiatives.
  • Support the Chief Executive Officer or directly represent the CEO in meetings with partners, suppliers, and government authorities.
  • Identify, build, and strengthen strategic partnerships to expand business opportunities and growth potential.

4 – Compliance Assurance

  • Closely monitor operations to ensure full compliance with applicable laws, regulations, and internal policies of the organization.
  • Proactively identify operational, procedural, and legal risks or bottlenecks, and recommend appropriate solutions.

5 – Administrative Support

  • Manage the Chief Executive Officer’s schedule, including meetings, appointments, and events, ensuring efficiency and effective coordination.
  • Prepare and manage presentations, meeting agendas, reports, and post-meeting action plans.

6 – Other Assigned Duties

  • Perform other tasks as assigned by the Chief Executive Officer to ensure smooth and effective operations of the organization.

Requirements:

1. Education

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in Economics, Business Administration, Finance, Marketing, International Relations, or related fields.
  • Graduates from top-tier universities (e.g. Foreign Trade University, Diplomatic Academy of Vietnam, National Economics University) are preferred.

2. Year of Experience

  • At least 02 years of experience as a Senior Executive Assistant (to CEO/Chairman) or expert in Business Development, Project Management, or similar roles.

3. Professional Knowledge

  • Strong interest and solid understanding of Marketing, Communications, Entertainment, and Music is preferred.

4. Skills

  • Strong planning, time management, and multitasking skills.
  • Project coordination and project management capability.
  • Analytical thinking, data handling, and report writing skills.
  • Excellent communication and relationship-building skills.
  • Proactive problem-solving with flexibility and efficiency.
  • Professional-level English proficiency (spoken and written).

5. Personal Attributes

  • Good business acumen with strong awareness of market context.
  • Systematic and logical thinking.
  • Proactive, reliable, highly confidential, and able to perform effectively in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment.

Benefits:

  • Competitive income and benefits based on capabilities.
  • Lunch allowance: 50.000 VNĐ/day
  • Provide laptop support for work.
  • Salary review twice a year, 13th-month salary bonus, holiday bonuses, etc.
  • Enjoy benefits according to State regulations (health insurance, social insurance, unemployment insurance).
  • Receive company welfare benefits such as birthdays, illness, and personal events.
  • 12 days of annual leave, public holidays.
  • Annual company trips, charity trips , and other internal activities.
  • Young, comfortable, and professional working environment.
  • Training provided by the Management team.

Working hours and Work Location:

  • 8:30 AM – 5:30 PM, Monday to Saturday (off 2 Saturdays per month)
  • 49 Trung Liet, Dong Da, Ha Noi
